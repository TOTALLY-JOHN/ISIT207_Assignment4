TABLE TENNIS CLUB NEWS

                   

   


Inti Subang College holds "Charity Tournament".

This club in Inti College Subang is to hold the tournament of the table tennis. If possible, some people from other universities are able to take part in this tournamnet with RM5 and possess the racket. For the winner of the tournament, the whole amount of money would be donated with the name of the winner. Besides, one certificate of winner would be offered. In the case that you would like to inquire, please send your email to the club of Inti Table Tennis. As soon as possible, we can make a reply quickly.

N.W.T. and Yukon train for table tennis

The session of table tennis club would be held in Ecole Whitehorse Elementary School. Yukon Canada Winder Game team welcomed the team so as to represent the Northwest Regions. At this game, the coach of Yukon has revealed this. Therefore, the professionals as well as coaches from Yukon and the territories in the Northwest side took a picture as getting their training. Of course, this training was totally difficult. However, it was totally satisfactory to everybody.

Table Tennis Networker Award

Youngsters in the age of between 15 and 18 are able to gain TT Networker Table Tennis England Award of leadership. Of course, some aims exist for some purposes. First and foremost, we would like to encourage TT Networkers to allow the organization to be more enjoyable and meaningful Table Tennis Clubs concentrating on primarily young people. Besides, we want to motivate people to be more active for coaching and forming festivals.
